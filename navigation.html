EV Navigator

Navigation

Once you enter your desired destination, the map will show you directions from your current location. The application will determine the distance, time, and average speed that your car will have to travel to/ with and notify you whether your car has enough energy to reach there.

If your car cannot reach to the location with the current conditions, then you will be given an option to locate nearby charging stations for charging your EV, before starting your journey.
